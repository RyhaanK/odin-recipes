Cannoli Recipe

Description

Cannoli is a delicious Italian dessert with crispy pastry shells filled with a creamy, sweet ricotta filling.

It's a beloved treat with a delightful texture and flavor.

Ingredients

Cannoli filling

  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cannoli Shell

(Can be prebought at italian markets or supermarkets)

  • 1 and a 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unstalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces

Steps

Cannoli Shells

  1. If making homemade shells, combine flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Cut in cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs
  2. Add egg yolk and roll out the dough thinly an cut into circles. Wrap each circle around a mould
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pot or deep fryer and fry the shells until golden and crisp.

Cannoli Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined and smooth.
