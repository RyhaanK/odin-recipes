Ramen Recipe

Description:

Ramen is a popular Japanese dish consisting of wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth.
It offers a comforting combination of tender noodles, savory broth, and a variety of toppings like chashu, soft-boiled eggs, and scallions.
With its customizable nature, ramen provides a satisfying and comforting dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups of sliced cooked protein (of your choice)
  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 1 cup of sliced scallions
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms (shiitake or enoki)
  • 1/2 cup of sliced bamboo shoots
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • Chilli paste (adjust to taste)

Steps
