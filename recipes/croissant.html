Croissant Recipe

Description

Croissant is a famous French pastry known for its flaky, buttery layers and crescent shape. It has a golden, crispy exterior and a soft, airy interior. Croissants can be enjoyed plain or filled with various sweet or savory fillings.

Ingredients

  • 3 and 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1 and 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softned
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Gradually add wamr milk and softened butter to the mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic
  4. Shape the dough into a ball, cover, and let it rise for around an hour until doubled in size
  5. Roll out the dough in a large rectangle on a floured surface
  6. Fold the rectangle into thirds like a letter
  7. Placed the shaped croissants on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  8. Cover and let them rise at room temperature for 1-2 hours until they increase in size
